Chicken Wrap Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 medium flour tortillas
  • 1 chicken breast (grilled, baked, or fried)
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or your favorite dressing
  • Optional: Sliced avocado, pickles, or hot sauce

Recipe

  1. Cook the chicken breast until fully done (grill, bake at 375Â°F for 20 minutes, or pan-fry). Slice it into strips.
  2. Lay the tortilla flat. Spread mayonnaise or dressing evenly.
  3. Add shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese in the center of the tortilla.
  4. Place sliced chicken strips on top. Add any optional toppings like avocado or hot sauce.
  5. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward, then roll it tightly from the bottom up.
  6. Slice the wrap in half and serve immediately.
