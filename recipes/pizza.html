Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20 slices of pepperoni

Recipe

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Place the pizza base on a baking sheet or pizza pan.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  6. Place the pizza in the preheated oven.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let the pizza cool for a minute or two, then slice and serve.
